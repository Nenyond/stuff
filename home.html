Who we are

The Buffalo Battletech community is a group of friendly and inclusive gamers that share a passion for the Battletech Universe and the associated games. We actively play Classic Battletech and Alpha Strike regularly on our game nights and host frequent special events.

The Game

Battletech: the Game of Armored Combat is a tabletop strategy game set in the distant future of the thirty-first century. Players command the pinnacle of human battlefield engineering, the Battlemech; a twelve meter tall nuclear powered tank with legs. The Great Houses of the Inner Sphere clash across war torn planets while beset by the invading Clans.
The game revolves around conflicts between these mechanical giants, either in Battlemech versus Battlemech duels or up to company scale engagements of about a dozen or so models per player.

The Hobby

The miniature painting hobby is a rich and rewarding experience. We host occasional hobby painting clinics where our in house hobby experts offer advice and teach various hobby topics. The Facebook Group and Discord channel are both great places to seek hobby advice and seek inspiration from our talented group of hobbyists. There is also the Gallery where you can find even more pictures of hobby projects from our community.

Our Events

The Buffalo Battletech community meets for regular game nights, usually on Thursday nights at Kingpin Comics & Gaming and on Tuesday nights at Madhouse Gaming & Hobby. See our Events Calendar for the current schedule of gaming events.
